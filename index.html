Jamiu Idowu is an AI Architect and Researcher specializing in AI Governance, AI Risk Management, LLM Evaluations, and Applied AI. He is the co-founder of Sahel AI, where he works with organizations to operationalize enterprise AI governance across the entire AI lifecycle. His research work is focused on AI Safety and Multi-Agent Systems. He is a Chevening Scholar and holds an MSc in AI (Distinction) from UCL. As a consultant, he has delivered solutions for global companies like Innovaccer, HP, New Relic, and JumpCloud.
